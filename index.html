What is the DuPont analysis?
The dupont analysis is a framework for analyzing the fundamental performance of a business popularized by the dupont corporation.
How to calculate DuPont analysis?
To calculate DuPont analysis you should use the following formula:
DuPont analysis = net profit margin * asset turnover * equity multiplier
where:
net profit margin = net income / revenue
asset turnover = sales / average total assets
equity multiplier = average total assets / average shareholder's equity
Why use DuPont analysis?
The DuPont analysis is sued to decompose the different drivers of return on equity and the decomposition of the return on equity give the apportonity for investors to focus more on the financial performance to identify strenghts and weaknesses.
